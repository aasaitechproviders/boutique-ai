FAQ

Straight answers

📱Do my customers need to download an app?

No. They just tap your link — it opens in any browser on their phone or laptop. No download, no login required for them. They enter their mobile number and start trying on.

⚡How fast are the try-on images?

Typically 6–12 seconds depending on the quality tier. Low quality is fastest. High quality takes slightly longer but shows stunning fabric detail and a two-panel full-body result.

💳What happens when I run out of credits?

Your customers will see a friendly message that try-ons are temporarily unavailable. You can top up from ₹500 anytime from your dashboard — credits are added instantly after payment.

🛍️Can I manage multiple boutiques?

Yes. Each agent is a separate boutique with its own catalog, URL, and chat history. Grower plan supports 3 agents. Enterprise supports unlimited — perfect for multi-branch or franchise owners.

🔗Can I use my own domain name?

Yes, on Grower and Enterprise plans you can connect a custom domain like tryon.yourstore.com. We'll walk you through the one-step DNS setup — takes about 2 minutes.

🖼️How good are the try-on images really?

Very good. We use Google Gemini's multimodal image AI. On High quality, you get a full-body + close-up two-panel shot with accurate fabric, color, and embroidery detail.

🔒Are customer photos stored safely?

Yes. All images are stored in encrypted AWS S3 buckets. Customers can delete their base photo anytime. We never share or use images for training.

🆙Can I upgrade or cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade from Starter to Grower any time from your dashboard. There's no lock-in — you keep your credits regardless of plan changes.

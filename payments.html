❓Frequently Asked Questions
What happens when I run out of credits?
Your agent stays live but new try-on requests are paused until you top up. Credits never expire — top up anytime with the packages below.
Is my payment secure?
Yes — payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your card details. You can pay via UPI, cards, net banking, or wallets.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes — Grower and Enterprise plans support custom domains. Once upgraded, go to Account Settings to add your domain and point a CNAME record.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, Paytm, and all major wallets — anything supported by Razorpay.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Upgrades take effect immediately after payment. For downgrades or cancellations, contact us and we'll sort it out right away.

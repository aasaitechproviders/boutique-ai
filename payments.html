❓Frequently Asked Questions
What happens when I hit my try-on limit?
Your agent stays live but new try-on requests are paused until your usage resets at the start of the next month. You can upgrade anytime to immediately increase your limit.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes — Grower and Enterprise plans support custom domains. Once upgraded, go to Account Settings to add your domain and point a CNAME record.
Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Contact us to change your plan and we'll adjust it right away.
What payment methods do you accept?
Currently we accept UPI, bank transfer, and Razorpay. Online payments will be integrated into the dashboard soon.
